Our Mission

We are the MMMAC Daddies! (No Affiliation With Mac Daddy’s).

Did you know, 1/5 MAC things are forgotten and lonely? There are MAC items that need a loving home. That's where we come in! We pair up our wonderful members with MAC items in need. From abandoned MacBooks to discarded macaroons, our mission is to find forever homes for any MAC in need. Our members and your donations make this all possible!

Together, We can do Great Things

There are MAC items everywhere that could use your help. Together, we can make sure they are all safe and sound in loving homes.
